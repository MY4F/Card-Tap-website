What's a SMART business card ?

Easily, just a tap to share your digital profile with anyone, anytime, anywhere! Link your digital profile to your CardTap profile and start sharing your profile in no time. This is all made possible by NFC technology. It is available in most Android and IOS phones.

Networking and connections are our first concern, engaging with clients and leaving them with a memorable experience and unforgettable ones, as saving your contact information directly into their mobile after your meeting ends, in an easy way. Raising your opportunities, positive influence, connections and mainly your confidence is our main objective.

Dealing with our card is very simple, by swipping the card above your mobile if you are an IOS user or below it if you are an Android user, then tap the pop-up link, to open your business profile or your website. It's an opportunity for all indviduals, not only for business owners, businessmen, startups, companies or entrepreneurs.
